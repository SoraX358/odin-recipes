Roasted Potatoes

Description:

The best potatoes ever made.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs of small, red potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3/4 teaspoons of kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoons of freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons of fresh, minced rosemary

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400oF.
  2. Thoroughly wash potatoes and let dry.
  3. Mince garlic cloves and rosemary very finely.
  4. Cut potatoes into halves or quarters and place in a bowl.
  5. Add olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic, and rosemary.
  6. Toss potatoes and mix until all potatoes are well-coated.
  7. Place potatoes on a baking sheet and spread out evenly into a single layer.
  8. Roast potatoes for 30 minutes.
  9. Take baking sheet out of the oven and flip potatoes over.
  10. Roast potatoes for 15 minutes.
  11. Take baking sheet out of the oven and flip potatoes over.
  12. Roast potatoes for 15 minutes or until browned and crisp.
  13. Let potatoes cool off for 5 minutes before serving.
