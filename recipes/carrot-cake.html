Carrot Cake

Description:

The best type of cake ever made.

Ingredients:

Cake:

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons of ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of fine, iodized salt
  • 3 cups of grated carrots
  • 1 cup of chopped pecans

Frosting

  • 4 cups of confectioners' sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 8 ounces of soft cream cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350oF (175oC).
  2. Grease and flour a 9x13-inch pan.
  3. Beat sugar, oil, and vanilla together in a large bowl until well combined.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
  5. Stir in carrots and pecans.
  6. Pour into the prepared pan.
  7. Bake for approximately 40 minutes (toothpick inserted into the center of the cake should come out clean).
  8. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
  9. Turn the cake onto a wire rack and let cool completely.
  10. Beat butter, cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, and vanilla in a large bowl. Resulting frosting should be smooth and creamy.
  11. Stir in chopped pecans.
  12. Apply frosting onto the cake.
  13. Garnish with additional pecans if desired.
